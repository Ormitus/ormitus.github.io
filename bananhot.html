Bananhot Sunny City

A complete visual identity for the "Sunny City" pop-up event by Bananhot, an Israeli swimwear brand by Neta Alchemister — logo design, colour palette, postcard series, and an illustrated city map, created with Persona Studio.

01
Colour Palette

Pastel
Summer

A warm, sun-soaked palette drawn from beach aesthetics — dusty blue skies, sea-foam teal, sun-kissed pink, and pale lemon yellow.

Each colour was selected to evoke a specific hour of the beach day — morning haze, midday shimmer, afternoon warmth, evening light. Together they form a palette that feels both contemporary and nostalgic.

02
Logo Design

Identity
System

The logo centres on a palm tree icon with "SUNNY CITY" arching above and "BANANHOT" anchored below in bold serif — evoking a summer resort destination.

The mark was developed through multiple sketching rounds, exploring the balance between the illustrative palm and the typographic weight of the wordmark. The final version achieves a classic resort-brand feel with a contemporary serif treatment.

03
Process

Logo
Exploration

From hand-drawn concepts to refined vectors — exploring typographic relationships and palm-tree interpretations before arriving at the final mark.

04
Print Design

Postcard
Series

A set of five postcards for the pop-up experience — each capturing a different summer mood, distributed as collectible keepsakes from Sunny City.

The postcards blend editorial photography with bold BANANHOT typography, creating a series that doubles as both marketing material and collectible art object.

About the Project

Bananhot
Swimwear

Bananhot is an Israeli swimwear brand by Neta Alchemister. The "Sunny City" pop-up was conceived as an immersive brand world — a fictional resort city where every touchpoint, from the logo to the city map, reinforced the brand's sun-drenched identity.

The project was a collaboration between Ori Mor and Persona Studio, combining graphic design, illustration, and print production into a cohesive brand experience.
