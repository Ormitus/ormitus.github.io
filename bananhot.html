Logo Design

Identity
System

The logo centres on a palm tree icon with "SUNNY CITY" arching above and "BANANHOT" anchored below in bold serif — evoking a summer resort destination. Designed to work across print, merchandise, and digital.

The mark was developed through multiple sketching rounds, exploring the balance between the illustrative palm and the typographic weight of the wordmark. The final version achieves a classic resort-brand feel with a contemporary serif.

Process

Logo Exploration
& Sketches

From hand-drawn concepts to refined vectors — the design process explored various typographic relationships and palm-tree interpretations before arriving at the final mark.

Print Design

Postcard
Series

A set of five postcards designed for the pop-up experience — each capturing a different summer mood. Visitors could collect and take them home as keepsakes from Sunny City.

The postcards blend editorial photography with bold BANANHOT typography, creating a cohesive series that doubles as both marketing material and collectible art object.

Illustration

The Sunny City
Map

An illustrated map guiding visitors through the pop-up's nine zones — from City Gate (entrance) to the Photo Booth. Printed and distributed on-site to create a full immersive experience.

The map's nine locations:

1. City Gate — Entrance
2. Main Avenue — Collection Area
3. Swim District — Swimwear Zone
4. The Changing Suites — Fitting Rooms
5. The Market — Checkout
6. The City Post Office — Pickup & Packaging
7. The Gift Shop — Sun Treasures
8. Sunny Square — Central Plaza
9. Photo Booth — The Postcard Spot

About the Project

Bananhot
Swimwear

Bananhot is an Israeli swimwear brand by Neta Alchemister. The "Sunny City" pop-up was conceived as an immersive brand world — a fictional resort city where every touchpoint, from the logo to the city map, reinforced the brand's sun-drenched identity.

The project was a collaboration between Ori Mor and Persona Studio, combining graphic design, illustration, and print production into a cohesive brand experience.
