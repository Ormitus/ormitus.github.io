Overview

The Solution for Moving

Moving day has never been simpler.

ARGO is a mobile application that connects people who are moving with professional movers, providing a complete solution for every stage of the moving process — from the initial search all the way through to the end of the move.

We connect you with trusted movers suited for every task — fast, simple, and at a fair price. Our movers are personally matched for heavy loads, crane services, special items, and more.

Problem Identification

Why do we need this?

Three core pain points in today's moving experience

01
Uncertainty
It's hard to compare prices and services, and the final price can change on moving day. Quotes are given over the phone based on rough estimates — "approximately what's in the apartment" — with no way to contest it.
02
Emotional Overwhelm
Moving is not just a logistical process — it's a significant emotional experience. Moving day brings enormous stress, fear, and uncertainty: How long will this take? Where are the movers? Did they take everything? What if something breaks?
03
Fragmented Platforms
Most of the moving process today happens offline or across many different platforms simultaneously. People send furniture photos to each mover via WhatsApp and Facebook — exhausting, time-consuming, and chaotic.

User Research

Who will use ARGO?

Moving personas — three archetypes representing the core user base

01
The Student Moving to the City
Doesn't have a lot of time and is looking for a solution that makes the move easier. Doesn't have much money and wants the best possible quote. Prioritizes speed and transparency above everything else.
02
The Young Couple
Their schedule is packed, and they need a fast, simple, and convenient solution. No time for delays — they prefer to pay someone to handle everything end-to-end. Values reliability and seamless communication throughout.
03
The Established Family
Looking for a service that gives them peace of mind and can handle any possible challenge. They have a lot of valuable, sensitive, and large items. Prioritizes trust, safety, and a truly full-service experience.

Flow Mapping

Where does ARGO fit in?

Mapping the complete user journey — from first search to move completion

01
Enter Initial Details & Find a Mover
The user enters move details first, and receives a list of only the movers who are available and work in their area — eliminating long calls that go nowhere before any real information is exchanged.
02
Scan the Home
Scan the home's contents and create a comprehensive list of the items being moved. Save time and effort — one scan generates an accurate, itemized inventory. No more sending photos over WhatsApp to multiple movers in parallel.
03
Receive Accurate Quotes & Choose a Mover
Receive precise price quotes from multiple movers and choose the best fit. Transparent pricing breakdown — you know exactly what to expect, with no surprises and no hidden costs.
04
Book the Move
Select your preferred move time and enter payment details. Simple, secure checkout that locks in the confirmed price before moving day arrives.
05
Moving Day — Real-Time Updates
Receive updates on the progress of the move and track in real time. Stay informed throughout the day: where are the movers now, what's been loaded, and when will they arrive at the new address?

Design Decisions

Pain Points & How We Solved Them

How ARGO directly addresses each friction point in the moving journey

Step 01
Mover Search
Long, Irrelevant Phone Calls
"I spent hours calling movers, only to find out they don't work in my area or aren't available on my date."
The user enters move details first and receives a list of only the movers who are available and work in their area — eliminating all the wasted calls upfront. No more calling around blindly.
Step 02
Home Scan
Disorder & Scattered Communication
"I had to send photos of every piece of furniture to each mover separately. Everything was in WhatsApp and Facebook, across multiple parallel conversations. Exhausting and time-consuming."
ARGO's home scanning feature creates a comprehensive inventory in minutes. One scan, shared automatically with all relevant movers — multi-platform coordination eliminated entirely.
Step 03
Pricing
The Price Always Changes on Moving Day
"The quote changed on the actual day of the move. The price was set over the phone based on a rough estimate of 'approximately what's in the apartment.' There was no way to dispute it."
Quotes are generated from the actual scanned inventory — itemized and locked in before the move begins. Transparent pricing breakdown per item category. No more unpleasant surprises.
Step 04
Moving Day
Emotional Overwhelm on the Day Itself
"Moving day is emotionally charged — enormous stress, fear, and uncertainty. How long will this take? Where are the movers right now? Did they take all my things? What if something breaks?"
Real-time tracking and continuous updates throughout moving day. Know exactly where your movers are at every moment. Checklists keep everything organized so nothing gets forgotten or left behind.
