The Concept

60 Years of
Dialogue

A mark that carries history, humanity, and hope.

The logo incorporates the number 60 in a design that reflects dialogue and collaboration between two nations. The numeral forms a ribbon — a universal symbol of solidarity — interweaving the colors of both flags: Israel blue and the German tricolor of black, red, and gold.


The logo also addresses the issue of the hostages, drawing inspiration from the solidarity expressed by German football fans, who highlighted this humanitarian cause with profound compassion and support. The ribbon form — traditionally associated with awareness and solidarity — carries this dual message: celebration of 60 years, and a call for human dignity.


Throughout this milestone year, the logo accompanies all joint activities — diplomatic, cultural, and civic — hosted by both the German and Israeli embassies, serving as the official emblem of this significant relationship.
